HK Express (Hong Kong Express Airways) is Hong Kong’s only homegrown low-cost carrier (LCC). Fully owned by the Cathay Pacific Group, the airline provides budget-friendly, no-frills air travel across the Asia-Pacific region. Operating under the slogan "Gotta Go," it is based at its main hub at [Hong Kong International Airport](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HK_Express).
## Core Profile and Fleet

* Business Model: A strict low-cost carrier focusing on customized journeys where passengers pay only for the services they need (such as checked baggage or inflight meals).
* The Fleet: Features a modern, narrow-body fleet consisting exclusively of the Airbus A320 family (including the A320, A320neo, A321, and A321neo).
* Safety & Reliability: Holds a consistent 7-Star Flight Safety Rating from AirlineRatings.com and ranks among the top low-cost carriers globally for on-time performance.

## Flight Network
HK Express connects Hong Kong to over 30 popular destinations across Asia. It specializes in leisure travel to regional hotspots, offering high-frequency flights to:

* Japan & South Korea: Major hubs including Tokyo, Osaka, Fukuoka, and Seoul.
* Greater China: Expanding routes into the Chinese Mainland (e.g., Ningbo, Yiwu, Beijing) and Taiwan.
* Southeast Asia: Vacation destinations throughout Thailand, Vietnam, Malaysia, and the Philippines.
